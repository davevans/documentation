Temporal is an open-source platform that makes your applications reliable and durable by default.

Built for developers, designed for scale. 
Temporal is code-first, fault-tolerant, and runs seamlessly across any infrastructure. 
With native support for over six general-purpose programming languages, Temporal eliminates complexity in building and managing distributed applications.

Thousands of companies of all sizes are leveraging Temporal's capabilities for both mission critical and standard workloads.
